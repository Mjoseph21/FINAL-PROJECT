      Vincent van Gogh's Father

           Theodorus van Gogh

Born in Benschop, Utrecht, Nederland on 8 February 1822 to Johannes Jansz van Gogh and Johanna van der Vin. Theodorus Van Gogh married Anna Cornelia Van Gogh and had 3 children. He passed away on 26 March 1885 in Nuenen, Nuenen, Gerwen en Nederwetten Municipality, Noord-Brabant, Netherlands.

        Vincent van Gogh's Mother

          Anna Carbentus van Gogh

Anna Cornelia Carbentus died on 29 april 1907. In her life she lost 4 sons. In 1852 she lost her first son Vincent in giving birth. She lost the the now famous painter Vincent van Gogh in 1890.
